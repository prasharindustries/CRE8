Fixing How Creators Get Paid

CRE8 is a decentralized payment protocol designed to fix one of the most broken systems on the internet: how creators get paid. Today, creators generate billions in value, yet they don’t control how or when they get paid. Platforms act as intermediaries, taking a significant cut while delaying payouts by weeks or even months.

CRE8 replaces this with programmable, instant payments. Using smart contracts, revenue is automatically split and distributed the moment a transaction occurs—no intermediaries, no delays, no ambiguity.

We’re not building another creator platform. We’re building the financial infrastructure that powers the next generation of them.

Why CRE8 Exists

The creator economy is growing exponentially, yet its financial infrastructure remains outdated and centralized. Creators still rely on platforms that control monetization, enforce delayed payout cycles, and lack transparency.

Even in Web3, most solutions focus on ownership while ignoring payment execution. Revenue distribution remains manual, inefficient, and prone to disputes.

CRE8 introduces a decentralized payment layer that automates revenue sharing at the protocol level. Funds are distributed instantly and transparently based on predefined logic.

  • 🚀 No intermediaries
  • ⚡ Instant payments
  • 🔐 Trustless execution
  • 📊 Transparent revenue flow

How It Works

CRE8 is built on Polygon using Solidity smart contracts that automate revenue distribution. Payments are split and executed automatically at the transaction level, eliminating manual intervention.

The current implementation supports automated revenue sharing between a creator and collaborator, with extensibility for multi-party distribution and dynamic percentages.

Integrated with MetaMask and powered by Ethers.js, CRE8 ensures seamless blockchain interaction while maintaining a simple and intuitive user experience.

Unlike traditional systems, CRE8 focuses on cash flow execution—ensuring money moves instantly and fairly.
