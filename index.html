About CRE8

CRE8 is a blockchain-based system designed to eliminate manual payment distribution in the creator economy.

It ensures instant, transparent, and trustless payments between creators and collaborators.

How It Works

1. User connects MetaMask wallet

2. Enters creator & collaborator addresses

3. Sends payment

4. Smart contract splits automatically (80/20)
