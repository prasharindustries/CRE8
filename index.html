Reimagining Creator Payments

CRE8 is a blockchain-powered system that automates how creators and their teams get paid.

Traditional platforms handle revenue generation but not distribution. CRE8 fills that gap with automated smart contract-based payments.

The Problem

  • Manual payouts create delays
  • No transparency in team payments
  • Trust-based system leads to disputes
  • No automated revenue splitting mechanism

The Solution

  • Smart contract-based automation
  • Instant revenue splitting
  • Transparent blockchain transactions
  • No intermediaries

About CRE8

CRE8 is built using Solidity and deployed on Polygon to provide a scalable and low-cost solution for creator payments.

Creator Economy

The creator economy is rapidly expanding, but its financial infrastructure remains outdated. CRE8 introduces automation and transparency.
