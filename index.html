Reimagining Creator Payments

CRE8 is a blockchain-based system designed to automate revenue distribution in the creator economy. It eliminates delays, removes intermediaries, and ensures instant, trustless payouts.

Purpose

CRE8 introduces a decentralized financial layer for creators by leveraging blockchain-based automation.

  • 🚀 Eliminate intermediaries
  • ⚡ Instant payments
  • 🔐 Trustless smart contracts
  • 📊 Transparent revenue sharing

About CRE8

Built on Polygon using Solidity smart contracts, CRE8 enables automated revenue splitting (80/20 model).

Unlike Web2 platforms, payments are executed instantly via smart contracts — no delays, no manual distribution.

Integrated with MetaMask and powered by Ethers.js for seamless blockchain interaction.
