Fixing How Creators Get Paid

A decentralized payment infrastructure for creators built on Polygon. CRE8 automates transparent revenue distribution using smart contracts, eliminating intermediaries, payout delays, and trust issues.

CRE8 is a blockchain-powered creator payment protocol designed to modernize revenue sharing across the creator economy.

Instead of relying on centralized platforms that delay payouts and take significant commissions, CRE8 enables instant and programmable payment execution directly through smart contracts.

Revenue is automatically distributed at transaction time with transparent, trustless, and deterministic execution.

Why CRE8 Exists

The creator economy generates billions in value, but its payment infrastructure remains centralized, opaque, and inefficient.

Platforms control monetization, enforce delayed payout cycles, and create unnecessary intermediaries between creators and revenue.

Even within Web3, many projects focus heavily on ownership and tokenization while ignoring the actual execution of payments.

CRE8 solves this problem through programmable on-chain revenue distribution using Solidity smart contracts on Polygon.

Technology & Engineering

CRE8 is implemented as a decentralized application focused on programmable financial execution rather than speculative tokenization.

The current proof-of-concept demonstrates automated creator-collaborator revenue splitting through blockchain transactions executed entirely on-chain.

The architecture is modular and extensible, enabling future support for:

  • Multi-party payment distribution
  • Dynamic revenue percentages
  • Subscription-based payouts
  • Platform integrations
  • Cross-platform creator monetization

The system emphasizes execution, transparency, and scalability while maintaining a simple and accessible user experience.
